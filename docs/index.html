Why should you use us?

Importance of a website

A website is how you present your business to the world, even in this age of social media, a business Facebook page is not enough, potential and existing customers will expect you to have a website and most will check it out before making a decision to further engage with you.

A quality website shows the world that you are serious, established professionals and have invested in getting that message across. A poor website could send the opposite message and even harm your companies image.

Frequently asked questions

Why are you more expensive than other agencies?

We have deliberately priced our services to reflect our considerable expertise, you will not be getting an off the shelf, templated WordPress website, you will be getting a hand coded, custom designed, beautiful, high-end website, from experts in our field and the price reflects that.

Will my website work on my phone / tablet?

Yes, absolutely, we'll make sure that your site looks beautiful, robust, and even native on mobile devices. we'll make your website downloadable (as an App on Android or via a home page link on iOS) and even work offline!

Can you make my website appear first in Google?

No, Search Engine Optimisation/Digital Marketing is not our speciality, however the website we build for you will be very search engine friendly, we'll ensure the appropriate meta data is in place and that best practices are followed, your site content will be very accessible to search engine crawlers, ready for an SEO expert to market.

Can you prove your websites are high quality?

Yes we can, we use Lighthouse, a built-in auditing tool in Chrome to check the performance, accessibility, best practices and SEO of all of our websites and ensure that no runtime are errors thrown, we encourage you to use the Google Chrome debug tools to look at competitors websites, see if there are any errors being thrown and run an audit against our sites and compare the results.

Can I update my website myself?

No, while it is certainly possible for you to update your website, it is best left to us to update your site for you under your ongoing support. We use a custom build tool to optimise your website for deployment and we have in place build steps which would need to be set up on your machine which would be too complex for non-developers.

Can I see how many visitors my site gets?

Yes, we'll set up Google Analytics on your site so you can see how many visitors you are getting, where they are located in the world and how they have reached your site. we'll set you up an account and show you how to use Google Analytics.

Getting a price

If you are interested in working with us, we'll send you a short survey to capture some basic information about your business, what you would like to say on the website and what your main goal is. Once we receive that, we'll then give you a quote detailing the upfront and monthly costs, our terms and conditions and a timeline for payment and delivery.

If you decide to proceed, we'll then ask that you sign a contract, agreeing to the initial and monthly costs and ask for 25% of the initial payment upfront and once received, we'll begin building your site.

Meeting your requirements

we'll set up a meeting, either face to face or video, depending on your location to kick off the project and make sure we are all on the same page.

During the build, we'll be in regular contact, we aim to get a design and skeleton site to you as quickly as possible, to ensure we are on the right track.

Delivery

Once we think the site is close to completion, we'll have another meeting, where we'll walk you through the site, demonstrating its features and gathering and applying any of your feedback, we want to make sure you are delighted with the end result.

Once signed off, we'll invoice you for the rest of the initial payment and ask that you set up a direct debit for the monthly payments. we'll release the website and send you a final survey to find out.
